Neapolitan Pizza

Authentic Italian Pizza

Neapolitan pizza, also known as Naples-style pizza, is a style of pizza made with tomatoes and mozzarella cheese.

The tomatoes must be either San Marzano tomatoes or Pomodorino del Piennolo del Vesuvio, which grow on the volcanic plains to the south of Mount Vesuvius

Ingredients

  • 600g Tipo 00 flour
  • 56% - 62% water of 600g @ 20 degrees C
  • 100g Salt
  • 50g Brown Sugar
  • 25g FRESH yeast

Steps

  1. Put flour in mixing machine
  2. Mix brown sugar and yeast toegther with water
  3. Pour 50% of water into bowl and mix until flour is moist but not dry
  4. Pour the rest of the water slowly and mix for 20 minutes
  5. The last 5 minutes slowly pour the brown sugar and yeast into bowl
  6. Take out mixed dough and let it sit in room temperature for 2 hours while it is covered
  7. Once the dough is fermented and has risen, keep the dough in fridge around 5 degrees C
