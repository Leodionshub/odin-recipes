Spinach Quesadilla

Vegetable Quesadilla

A superior quesadilla that will leave your mouth watering

Who would of thought that spinach would go good in a quesadilla? No meat? Yet it's delicious!

Ingredients

  • Large flour torillas
  • 100g Fresh spinach
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Cook spinach with cream and mozarella cheese until it's watery
  2. Put torilla on frying pan or grilla pan on medium heat
  3. Put spinach on one side of the circular flour torilla. Don't let it overflowover the edges
  4. Sprinkle some mozarella cheese on the half side of the circular tortilla
  5. fold torilla in half so it looks like a half moon
  6. flip torilla on other side when on side is cooked
  7. Cut the half moon shaped quesadilla into 4 pieces like a pizza
